Welcome to the Site!

Here at The Mountain Spoke we are excited to newly released site that has been underconstruction
for quite some time! Here you will be able to see what exactly we have to offer for all of our clients!
Whether you are a new customer or a returning customer, we appreciate the time that is being
taken to let us offer our services to you and help you with your new endeavors this mountain biking
season! Check us out on our newly renovated social media accounts for updates and new videos and
pictures posted everyday!

Say Hello to Tours!

We are excited to announce that we will not be offering tours! What that will include is a tour guide
that has professional experience in mountain biking. We are pleased to have many different
backgrounds of teaching styles with all of our various tour guides! They each have their favorite trail
that they will take you on. So get ready for rides that you will never forget.

No More Issues!

Another new option that we have added to the business is the fact that we will be offering bike repair
services! No longer is there a need to make sure that everything is all fixed before you come on the
trail. With the purchase of one of our new tours, you will have a $25 free service repair that can be used
before or after the trail ride. We are wanting to really listen to our customers and due to high request
for this, we have decided to go right ahead and offer this for all tours that are booked! Now let's get ready
and ride!
